## Components List


### TAG
- PETG/PLA 3D printed enclosure (top cover + backplate + assembly)
- **BLE module:** Raytac MDBT42Q-512KV2 / MDBT42Q-P512KV2 (nRF52832) — 1x
- **Battery:** CR2032 3 V lithium coin cell — 1x
- **CR2032 holder:** Single-cell PCB holder (e.g. HandsOn CON1118 or Keystone 1060/1061 style) — 1x
- **Magnetic reed switch:** Littelfuse MRPR-3 or generic miniature SPST-NO reed switch — 1x
- **Debug/status LED:** Low-current SMD LED, 0603 or 0805 (green or amber) — 1x
- **Decoupling capacitor:** 100 nF ceramic, X7R/X5R, 0603/0805 — 1x
- **Bulk capacitor:** 10–47 uF ceramic/tantalum, 0603/0805/1206 — 1x
- **SWD programming pads:** Tag-Connect footprint or 5 test pads (VDD, GND, SWDIO, SWDCLK, RESET) — 1x
- **3M VHB adhesive pad:** Double-sided foam tape, thin variant (~0.8–1.2 mm) — 1x
- **M2 screws:** For enclosure closure (2x)


### RECEIVER
- 3D printed haptic receiver clip
- **Dev board:** Seeed Studio XIAO nRF52840 BLE — 1x
- **LiPo battery:** 3.7 V 150–300 mAh protected LiPo (with JST-PH connector) — 1x
- **LiPo charger IC:** MCP73831/MCP73832 (deferred — XIAO handles charging in prototype)
- **Coin vibration motor:** Vybronics VC1030B028F or generic 10 mm × 3 mm 3 V motor — 1x
- **N-channel MOSFET:** AO3400 SOT-23 (low-side switch for vibration motor) — 1x
- **Flyback / protection diode:** 1N5819 Schottky or SMD SS14 equivalent — 1x
- **Power slide switch:** Mini SPDT slide switch (e.g. Würth 450302014072) — 1x
- **Main tactile button:** 6 × 6 mm tactile switch (e.g. C&K PTS645 or generic 6×6×5 mm) — 1x
- **M2 screws:** For enclosure closure (2x)
